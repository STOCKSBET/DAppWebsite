STBT is an Ethereum Smart Contract based DApp for betting on the price of Stocks and win from everyone who bets against you.

Users bet on a stocks that performs the best among all the other competing ones in a specific time period. Bets are placed and rewards are claimed using Metamask plugin for Chrome/Firefox or the Ethereum based Mist browser.

Being completely secured by the blockchain, no one has control over the race or user funds once a contract is deployed and a race is announced.
